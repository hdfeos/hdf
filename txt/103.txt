HDF and HDF-EOS Workshop XII
============================

NASA Goddard Space Flight Center's Earth Sciences Data and Information System
Project and The HDF Group are presenting the HDF and HDF-EOS Workshop XII to
discuss use of the HDF standards in satellite observations of the Earth and
related applications.

The location and date of the workshop are:

    Aurora, Colorado
    October 15-17, 2008

Please see the following page for complete details:

    http://www.hdfeos.org/workshops/ws12/workshop_twelve.php

The workshop will be held at the Doubletree Hotel in Aurora, CO and will
consist of presentations, discussions, tutorials and demonstrations of tools.
You can take a look at past years' workshops on the HDF-EOS Tools and Information
Center web site, at:

   http://www.hdfeos.org/

Suggestions for topics that you'd like to see on the agenda can also be
contributed and are welcomed.

The theme this year is:

  Moving Forward with HDF - New Missions, New Opportunities, a New Generation of Users

Bring your ideas, needs, successes, and challenges.

There will be a poster area. Presenters have the option of bringing a poster
and giving a 5 minute 'express' talk or giving a longer talk.

Please send an email with a few paragraphs describing your intent to present
or desired topic discussion to:   Daniel.J.Marinelli@nasa.gov
